Consider the following facts and represent them in predicate form.
F1: There are 500 employees in ABC Company
F2: Employees earning more than Tk. 250000 per annum pays tax.
F3: Jamal is a manager in ABC Company.
F4: Manager earns Tk. 500000 per annum.
Convert the facts in predicate form to clauses and then prove by resolution: ‘Jamal pays tax’.
